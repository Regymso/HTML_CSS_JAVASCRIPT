LIFE CYCLE

There are three important events in the lifecycle of a piece of data; creation, use and deletion. At all other times the data only represents risk. Many organizations apply ILM processes to structured data and paper records only; the open nature of unstructured file services leading to an intimidating level of dynamism in their content
